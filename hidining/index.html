ʻAlohilani Resort

Swell

Perched beside ʻAlohilani's iconic infinity pool, Swell is an unrivaled destination with sweeping ocean views of Waikīkī Beach. Sip craft cocktails in a private cabana, enjoy locally sourced small plates, and catch live music from local musicians and DJs.

Mon–Thu 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM

One of Waikīkī's most breathtaking pools — an infinity-edge oasis with panoramic ocean views stretching to the horizon. Lounge on a plush daybed, order poolside from Swell, and watch Waikīkī unfold beneath a blue Pacific sky.
